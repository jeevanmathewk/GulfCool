Single Split System

Single split air conditioners are ideal for targeted air conditioning or single rooms and spaces without multiple climate zones. But, compare split system A/Cs vs packaged systems to see which one is best.

  • Cost Effective
  • No windows are required.
  • Easy to install.

Multi-split System

Multi-split air conditioners are an excellent solution for multiple rooms and climate zones but suffer in terms of efficiency.

  • Convenient and economical.
  • Space-saving installation.
  • Independent control.
  • A multi split air conditioning unit is a great choice if you wish to air condition more than one location in your house and need to independently control the temperature of each room. It is also a great alternative to a ducted system if the space in the roof or under the floor is restricted.

Wall mounted

Split air conditioners don't require packing and unpacking when the summer season ends. A wall mounted air conditioner doesn't eat up space as it is typically positioned high on a wall.

  • Wall Air Conditioners do not Block the View.
  • Easy Maintenance
  • Wall Air Conditioners Do Not Cool Your Whole Home

Under sealing mounted

Underslung ceiling and ceiling suspended air conditioning are air conditioning unit types that are best suited to big spaces like business offices, server rooms and shops. The units suspend from or sit just below the ceiling, and are a good solution for rooms that don't have a suspended or fake ceiling.

  • High Efficiency
  • The units save valuable space within a business
  • Light Weight and Compact Indoor Unit Facilitating Easy Installation
  • Under ceiling units are comparatively quiet systems
  • The positioning of the units is considered one of the safest for employees

Floor Mounted

Floor standing air conditioners are air conditioners that are installed on or near the floor or recessed in spots like unused fireplaces. Floor standing air conditioners are easy to access and maintain and are popular in homes with little wall space or high ceilings.

  • Eco-Friendly AC Options .
  • Cost Savings.
  • Saves Wall Space.

Ducted Systems

Ducted air conditioners are common in the workplace, shopping centres and other large areas, but they are also increasingly common for the home. They involve a central location where the air is treated, then sent by fans through a system of ducts and into each room.

  • Whole home heating and cooling
  • Low profile looks
  • Efficient
  • Quiet operation
